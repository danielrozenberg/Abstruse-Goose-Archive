All Comics

Abstruse Goose was an anonymous webcomic about science, mathematics, and philosophy, published from around 2008 until the original site went offline in 2023. This archive was assembled from the Wayback Machine to preserve the strips.

A few things to know: some strips are missing — they were deleted from the original site or couldn't be recovered. The transcripts and visual descriptions shown for each comic were generated automatically by an AI and may contain errors or hallucinations. This web app was entirely vibe-coded.

Contributions are welcome — fixes to transcripts, descriptions, or the app itself can be submitted as pull requests on GitHub.
